Avocado Salad

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 small red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro (optional)

Recipe

  1. Cut the avocados, remove the pits, and dice the flesh.
  2. In a large bowl, combine avocado, cherry tomatoes, onion, and cucumber.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice.
  4. Season with salt, pepper, and toss gently.
  5. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve immediately.
