Butter Chicken

Ingredients

  • 500g boneless chicken, cut in pieces
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Coriander for garnish

Recipe

  1. Marinate chicken in yogurt, lemon juice, ginger-garlic paste, chili powder, and salt for at least 1 hour.
  2. Grill or pan-fry the chicken pieces until cooked.
  3. In a pan, melt butter, add tomato puree, and cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Add grilled chicken, cream, garam masala. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Garnish with coriander and serve hot with naan or rice.
