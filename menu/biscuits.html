Chocolate Biscuits

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 0.5 cup cocoa powder
  • 0.5 cup sugar
  • 0.5 cup butter
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 egg
  • Pinch of salt

Recipe

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Cream together butter and sugar until smooth.
  3. Add egg and mix well.
  4. Mix in flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt.
  5. Shape into small balls, place on baking tray, flatten slightly.
  6. Bake for 12-15 minutes. Cool, then serve.
