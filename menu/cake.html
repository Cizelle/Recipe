Cake

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1.5 cups sugar
  • 1 cup milk
  • 0.5 cup butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Recipe

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F). Grease a cake pan.
  2. Cream butter and sugar together until fluffy. Add eggs one at a time.
  3. Add vanilla extract.
  4. Mix flour, baking powder, and salt in a bowl.
  5. Add dry ingredients and milk, alternating, mixing gently.
  6. Pour into pan, bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  7. Let cool, then serve.
