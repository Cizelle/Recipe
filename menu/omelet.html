Omelet

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 2 tbsp milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • Chopped vegetables (tomato, onion, spinach) - optional
  • Grated cheese (optional)

Recipe

  1. Beat eggs with milk, salt, and pepper.
  2. Heat butter in a nonstick pan over medium heat.
  3. Pour in the eggs, cook gently, stirring a bit as it sets.
  4. Add vegetables and cheese if using.
  5. Fold omelet in half, cook for another minute. Serve hot.
